# Core Gameplay Validation Checklist

## Scope

Validate title flow, gameplay start, movement, firing, asteroid splitting, level progression, lives, pause/resume, and game over.

## Checklist

- [ ] Open the game and confirm the title screen is displayed.
- [ ] Press `Enter` and confirm gameplay starts.
- [ ] Rotate, thrust, and fire; confirm ship and projectiles behave correctly.
- [ ] Destroy a large asteroid and confirm it splits.
- [ ] Clear a level and confirm the next level starts.
- [ ] Lose lives and confirm the HUD updates.
- [ ] Pause with `Escape`, then resume with `Escape`.
- [ ] Reach game over and confirm the game-over screen appears.
