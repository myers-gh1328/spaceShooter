# Advanced Mechanics Validation Checklist

## Scope

Validate scoring/combo, extra life thresholds, power-ups, saucers, visibility pause, and leaderboard persistence.

## Checklist

- [ ] Destroy objects quickly enough to increase combo.
- [ ] Wait out the combo window and confirm combo resets.
- [ ] Cross an extra-life threshold and confirm a life is awarded.
- [ ] Observe or force each power-up type and confirm effect behavior.
- [ ] Confirm offensive power-up replacement works correctly.
- [ ] Confirm shield absorbs exactly one hit.
- [ ] Reach level 3+ and validate saucer spawn, fire, and scoring.
- [ ] Switch tab visibility and confirm auto-pause behavior.
- [ ] Finish a run with a score and confirm leaderboard persistence after reload.
